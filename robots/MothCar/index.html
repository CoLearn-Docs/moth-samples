MothCar

The MothCar is TeamGRIT's custom donkey car, which is based on the Jetson Nano.
It demonstrates strong performance in terms of streaming video and control using ROS.
We use our Moth server for transmitting/receiving real-time video in donkey car.
That's why we call it MothCar.
