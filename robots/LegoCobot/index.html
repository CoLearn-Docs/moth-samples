Lego Cobot

The Lego cobot uses the RPi Zero 2 W, which despite its compact size, guarantees outstanding performance.
Also, it can be extended to any form of robot by using Lego materials.
Users can create their own Lego robot. The Lego Car is also operated by Moth server.
